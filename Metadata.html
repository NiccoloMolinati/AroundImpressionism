Metadata Alignmet

The next step is the metadata analysis. With a more in depth research, we found (or in a couple of cases, decided to assign) the metadata schema used to describe each object.

Following the examination and characterization of the standards employed by the aforementioned institutions, we harmonized the standards, aligning their attributes based on their respective functions. Notably, we segregated the attributes into four primary categories corresponding to our item classification (Person, Date, Place, Event/concept, as delineated in our E/R model). A review of these tables reveals distinctive materials and objects for description by each institution, juxtaposed with the unique standards applicable to individual objects.
